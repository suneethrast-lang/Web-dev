Frequently Asked Questions

Standard check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM noon. Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request, subject to availability. Suite guests and members enjoy flexible timing as a complimentary benefit.
Complimentary breakfast is included with all Deluxe Rooms, Executive Suites, and Presidential Suites. Penthouse guests enjoy in-suite breakfast service prepared by a private chef. Breakfast is served daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM.
Yes — we offer complimentary luxury chauffeur-driven airport transfers for all suite and penthouse bookings. Standard room guests can arrange transfers at a preferential rate through our concierge. Please inform us of your flight details 24 hours in advance.
